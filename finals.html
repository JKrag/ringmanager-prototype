Finals

Select cats for ring finals

This page will probably contain a reasonably simple interface to quickly fill in the numbers of the (typically 10) cats to call for the the next final. After selecting or typing all 10, the user is returned to the central Judging screen with all cats entered directly in the "calling" state (second column).
